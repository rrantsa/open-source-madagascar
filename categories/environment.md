# Environment

This category lists open source projects related to biodiversity, climate, land use, conservation, environmental monitoring, and ecological data relevant to Madagascar.

## Included Projects

### Deforestation Maps Mada

- Repository: https://github.com/ghislainv/deforestation-maps-Mada
- Description: Reproducible research repository documenting sixty years of deforestation and forest fragmentation in Madagascar.
- Category: environment
- Additional categories: datasets
- Language: TeX, R, and GRASS GIS
- Framework: Reproducible research workflow
- License: CC-BY-SA-4.0
- Status: inactive
- Maintainer: ghislainv
- Target users: Researchers, conservation NGOs, environmental analysts, students
- Madagascar relevance: The repository is directly centered on Madagascar forest change and provides reusable environmental analysis assets with clear local scientific value.
- Notes: The work is highly valuable and reproducible, but the last visible code push was on 2020-11-25, so `inactive` is the safest status.
