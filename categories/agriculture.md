### Fataplus Tsena ny Tantsaha

- Repository: https://github.com/fenoh3ry/Fataplus-Tsena-ny-tantsaha
- Description: Mobile application for Malagasy farmers to access agricultural market prices ("Tsena ny Tantsaha" = farmer prices), built to connect rural producers with fair market information.
- Category: agriculture
- Language: Dart
- Framework: Flutter
- License: unknown
- Status: inactive
- Maintainer: fenoh3ry
- Target users: Farmers, agricultural cooperatives, NGOs working in rural Madagascar
- Madagascar relevance: Designed specifically for Malagasy farmers to access agricultural market prices — addresses the information asymmetry between rural producers and urban markets, a critical issue in Madagascar's agriculture sector.
- Notes: License not specified in the repository. No recent activity since March 2024. The concept addresses a real pain point — smallholder farmers lack real-time price transparency in Madagascar's agricultural supply chain.
