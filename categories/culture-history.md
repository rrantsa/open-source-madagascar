# Culture and History

This category will list open source projects related to archives, local history, heritage preservation, and cultural collections.

### Imaginarium

- Repository: https://github.com/fenoh3ry/imaginarium
- Description: AI-powered immersive platform for Malagasy cultural heritage education, featuring 7,237 ohabolana (Malagasy proverbs), RAG-powered chat, and auth-enabled learning experiences.
- Category: culture-history
- Language: Svelte
- Framework: SvelteKit, Cloudflare Workers, D1, Hono
- License: MIT
- Status: active
- Maintainer: fenoh3ry
- Target users: Students, teachers, researchers, Malagasy culture enthusiasts
- Madagascar relevance: The largest open collection of ohabolana (Malagasy proverbs) with AI-assisted exploration — a digital heritage preservation tool built by and for Malagasy. Preserves oral tradition through modern tech.
- Notes: Live at fable.fenohery.space. Cross-listed in language-ai for its NLP/RAG capabilities. The 7,237 ohabolana dataset is a unique cultural asset for Madagascar.
